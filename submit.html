Submit a track or club

Help build the directory. Submissions are reviewed before they appear on the map — usually within a few days. Got a local track or club we're missing? Fill in what you know.

What makes a good submission

Be accurate — especially the address. If the venue doesn't have a proper street address, give us the nearest cross street, town and postcode. Mention junior facilities, social ride days, or anything else that makes the place stand out.

Pick "Both" if it's a club that runs its own riding venue (like Appin MX Park).
Full street address with postcode. e.g. "Brian Rd, Appin NSW 2560". If there's no street address, the nearest landmark or cross-street with town and postcode is fine.
A few sentences — what's the track like, who runs it, what's it best for?
Comma-separated. e.g. Senior MX track, Junior track, Canteen, Camping
We'll review and email you back.
